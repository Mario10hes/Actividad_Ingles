📖 Written Description

Barrio La Madrid is a vibrant and welcoming neighborhood located in the south of Villavicencio, in the department of Meta, Colombia. The streets are organized in a grid pattern: horizontal calles run east to west, and vertical carreras run north to south. The main roads are Calle 91 Sur, Calle 92 Sur, and Calle 92A Sur, crossed by Carreras 52 through 57.


The neighborhood has everything a family needs. There is a large school in the north, a supermarket and pharmacy in the center, and a bakery and fast food restaurant towards the east. For recreation, residents enjoy the Polideportivo and a sand sports court in the west, and a beautiful community park in the east. A police station near Calle 92A Sur provides safety for all residents. The area has a friendly, community-oriented atmosphere.
